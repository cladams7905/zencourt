## Listing Subcategory: New Listing

### Objective

Launch a listing with high specificity and clear value.

### Required Direction

- Lead with availability plus one standout attribute.
- Include concrete facts from listing data (price, beds/baths, lot, upgrades, address) when available.
- Use a short value checklist style when it improves scannability.
- Add one direct next step CTA, for example DM for details or schedule a tour.
- Keep urgency honest and avoid pressure language.
