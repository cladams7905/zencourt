## Listing Subcategory: Price Change

### Objective

Frame a pricing update as a value opportunity without hype or pressure.

### Caption Structure

Use this structure for price change posts:

1. One line with the new price and brief framing of the update.
2. One line explaining why the home is compelling at the current value.
3. A short checklist of 3-5 key features that reinforce value at this price point.
4. Address block with `📍` prefix.
5. New price as a standalone emphasis line.
6. Single CTA focused on tour requests or details.

### Style Guidance

- Signal the price update clearly in the first two lines.
- Use exact pricing figures only when provided in listing data.
- Keep the tone confident and informative, not pushy or desperate.
- Frame as opportunity, not desperation.
- Preserve blank lines between sections for readability.
